IRCC Secure Account: Sign in

There are different accounts for various immigration and citizenship application forms, paying, checking status and making changes. Before you register or sign in, check when to use this secure account:

When to use this secure account

Use this account to:

  • review, check the status & read messages for applications you've submitted
  • start or continue an application for work and study permits, Express Entry, and International Experience Canada
    • you will need your personal reference code if you already have one
    • you can also submit and pay for your application, get messages related to your application, check the status of your application and update your information
    • start an application to extend your stay
  • if you're already a citizen: apply for a proof of citizenship certificate or search for your citizenship record
  • apply for the Interim Federal Health Program if you are a protected person or refugee claimant
  • transfer schools if you are an approved study permit holder
    • you will need your application number
  • apply for some types of visitor visas - get visitor visa instructions for your situation

Delays with the status of your medical exam results
If we asked you for a medical exam after you applied

You may not see the status of your results updated right away.

  • Your results usually show up in your account within 30 days of the date you booked your exam.
  • It may take longer (for example, if we need more information from you).
If you got your medical exam before you applied (upfront medical exam)

You may not see the status of your results updated in your account until we finish processing your immigration application. We need time to

  • process your application
  • link the results to your application

Check the application processing time.

If we ask or if a panel physician asks you for additional medical information, tests or treatment

To avoid more delays, you must

  • go back to see the physician
  • complete the requirement before we can assess your medical exam

Don’t complete a new medical exam unless we instruct you to do so.

Your panel physician can explain how long it will take to complete additional testing or treatment.

Sign in the same way every time you use your account:

  • the sign-in option you chose was tied with your account and user profile - if you choose a different one, you may be asked to register again

If you haven't already registered for an account, register now.

Forgot your GCKey password or username

Choose the instructions for your situation:

Forgot your username AND password

If you added a recovery email address to GCKey:

  1. Select the GCKey username and password button
  2. Select Forgot your username? on the Welcome to GCKey page to have it emailed to you
  3. Once you receive your username through email, select Forgot your password? on the Welcome to GCKey page and follow the steps to reset it
If you didn't add a recovery email address to GCKey:
  1. Select the GCKey username and password button
  2. Select the Sign up button on the Welcome to GCKey page to create a new GCKey username and password and register for a new account
  3. During this process you may be able to recover your account or link existing applications to your new account
Forgot your username
  1. Select the GCKey username and password button
  2. Select Forgot your username? on the on the Welcome to GCKey page
  3. Enter your recovery email address on the Recover Username Step 1 of 2 page:
    • you provided your recovery email address either when you signed up for your GCKey username and password or at a later time
    • if you didn't add a recovery email address and have forgotten your username and your password, select the Sign up button on the Welcome to GCKey page to create a new GCKey username and password, and register again
  4. Select Continue
    • You'll be brought to a page that explains where your username will be emailed to and what to do if you don't receive the email
  5. Select Continue again
  6. Check your email, you should receive an email from the Government of Canada with your GCKey username
  7. Sign in with your username and password on the Welcome to GCKey page
Forgot your password
  1. Select Forgot your password? on the Welcome to GCKey page
  2. Enter your GCKey username
  3. Select Continue
    • If you entered an email address when signing up for your GCKey username then you should be able to reset your password by entering that email address
    • If you didn't enter an email address when signing up then follow the instructions in the Recovery Questions and Answers box
Password reset using an email address
  1. If you entered your email address, check your email, you should receive an email from the Government of Canada with a confirmation code
  2. Enter the confirmation code you receive on the Forgot Your Password Step 2 of 4 page (this page should have automatically loaded for you after you entered your username and selected Continue on the Forget Your Password Step 1 page)
  3. Select Continue
  4. You'll be prompted with a recovery question
    • This is a question you would have answered when you first created a GCKey username and password
  5. Enter the answer to the question in the My Recovery Answer field
  6. Select Continue
  7. Enter a new password in the New Password field
  8. Re-enter that same password in the Confirm New Password field
  9. Select Continue
  10. Select Continue again on the Forget Your Password Complete page
Password reset using recovery questions
  1. On the Forget Your Password Step 2 of 4 page, select the Recovery Q&A button in the Recovery Questions and Answers box
  2. Enter your answers in the My Recovery Answer, My Memorable Person Answer and My Memorable Date fields (you should find the questions/hints above each field)
  3. Enter a new password in the Create Your New Password field
  4. Enter the same password in the Confirm Your New Password field
  5. Select Continue
  6. If you correctly answered all the recovery questions you'll be brought to a page called Forget Your Password Complete
  7. Select Continue
Change or add GCKey two-factor authentication

Once you have created your GCKey username and password you may be asked to choose a method of two-factor authentication. This is an enhanced security measure that helps protect your personal information.

On the Two-factor authentication page choose one of the authentication methods and follow the on-screen instructions.

Change your two-factor authentication

To change your two-factor authentication, you have three options:

  1. select Manage your Two-Factor Authentication on the Welcome page displayed after you sign in with your GCKey username and password
  2. or, use your existing two-factor authentication and select the Change your two-factor authentication button on the Authentication success page
  3. or, select Recover account on the Two-factor authentication page and enter one of the recovery codes you saved when you first set up two-factor authentication, and then select the Change your two-factor authentication button on the Authentication success page
    • if you lost or didn't save your recovery codes, start again and register for a new account with a new GCKey username, password
    • save your new set of recovery codes when you add two-factor authentication
    • during this process, you may be able to recover your account or link existing applications to your new account

If you need more help with two-factor authentication, call the GCKey Service Desk:

They can help you with technical issues related to GCKey two-factor authentication. They don’t have access to any Government of Canada account or application information.

GCKey username and password revoked or invalid

If you haven't signed in with your GCKey username and password at least once in the last 2 years, they have been revoked and removed. You'll need to:

  • register for the service again with a new GCKey username and password
  • during this process, you may be able to recover your account or link existing applications to your new account
Switch your Interac® Sign-in partner

If you've moved to a new bank or credit union, and already sign in through Interac® Sign-in Partner, you may be able to switch:

  • select Switch My Sign-in Partner from the menu on the Select a Sign-in Partner page

Browser errors and technical problems

You can use the same GCKey username and password on different computers, phones and tablets.

If you're getting error messages:

  • use an updated modern web browser like Safari, Chrome, Firefox or Microsoft Edge, rather than Internet Explorer
  • don't use a favourite link or bookmark to pages inside the account - you must sign in to see them
  • use your browser's default settings because GCKey needs JavaScript and cookies enabled
Messages when your browser is out of date

If you see one of these messages when you try to sign in, update your browser:

  • Page Can’t Be Displayed
  • Cannot Establish a Secure Connection
  • Page Not Available

You can usually find the option to update your browser in your browser menu's settings or preferences. If you are using Internet Explorer, update your browser to Microsoft Edge.

Other technical issues
  • Too many attempts:if you make too many unsuccessful attempts to sign in during the same session, you may see a failure message. Close your browser window or tab and start again.

  • "Experiencing technical difficulties": The site could be down for a number of reasons, including a brief period of system maintenance. We apologize for the inconvenience. Try again later.
  • Session expired:for security, your session will be closed if it's inactive for a certain number of minutes. Sign in again.
