My Service Canada Account (MSCA): Register for

The first time you access My Service Canada Account, you'll need to register to set up a username and password, prove your identity, and set up your account profile.

Don't register to apply for EI. Find the right way to apply for Employment Insurance benefits.

Find the right way to register for your situation

There are many choices to make when you register. Answer a few questions to help find the best options for you:

Did you receive a letter with your Personal Access Code (PAC) in the mail within the last x months?

  • Yes I received my Personal Access Code (PAC) in the mail

    Great. You will be able to use your Personal Access Code later at the step to verify your identity. Now we'll help you choose which username and password option you'll use for your account.

Sign up to GCKey to access ROE Web

You must sign up for a GCKey before you can register to ROE Web. Select "Start with GCKey" below and follow the sign-up instructions on the Welcome to GCKey page.

Once you have successfully signed up to GCKey, you will be redirected to the ROE Web portal to create your ROE Web account.

Start with GCKey

  1. Determine if your bank is a Sign-in Partner

    To register to ROE Web with your bank, you must have a Canadian online bank account with one of our Sign-In Partners.

    If your bank is not listed as a Sign-in Partner you must register to ROE Web using GCKey.
