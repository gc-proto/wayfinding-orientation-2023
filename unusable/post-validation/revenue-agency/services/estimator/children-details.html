Children details: Child and family benefits estimator

Do you have shared custody?

You have shared custody if

  • a child only lives with you part time and
  • the child shares their time about equally between the parents

Each parent must apply for Canada child benefit separately and the CRA will calculate the amount each parent will get separately, using their respective family income

Each parent will get exactly 50% of what they would have gotten if they had sole custody of the child, calculated based on their own income

The CRA will not split the amount using other percentages, or give the full amount to one of the parents.
