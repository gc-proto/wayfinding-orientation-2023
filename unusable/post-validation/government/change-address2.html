Personal change of address

For which services do you need to change your address?

Check all the services that might have a record of your address

  • Social Insurance Number (SIN)
  • Employment Insurance (EI), or parental, sickness or caregiving leave
  • Canada Pension Plan (CPP) or Old Age Security (OAS)
  • Personal taxes and Canada Child Benefit (CCB)
  • Immigration and visas
  • Nexus
  • Voting registration
  • Canada Savings Bonds
  • Health card or driver's license (with your province)
  • Pleasure craft licence

  • Veteran benefits or correspondence
