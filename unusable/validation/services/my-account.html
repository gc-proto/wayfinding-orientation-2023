Avoid technical problems in MSCA

To avoid technical problems in MSCA:

  • Don’t bookmark secure MSCA pages (the pages you see after you log in)
  • Use one of the following Web browser:
    • Google Chrome  (version 9 or newer)
    • Safari (version 5 or newer)
    • Internet Explorer (version 28 or newer)
    • Microsoft Edge (any version)
    • Mozilla Firefox (version 22 or newer)
  • Enable cookies and JavaScript in your browser

Enable cookies and JavaScript in your browser for MSCA

Enable cookies in:

  • Google Chrome
  • Safari on iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch
  • Safari on desktop
  • Internet Explorer and Edge
  • Mozilla Firefox

Enable JavaScript in :

  • Google Chrome
  • Safari
  • Internet Explorer and Edge
  • Mozilla Firefox

Note: MSCA doesn’t use pop-ups to ask for personal information.
