Direct deposit for businesses and other organizations

Most payments your business or organization receives from the Government of Canada are eligible for direct deposit. Find out how to sign-up or update your banking information.

Supplier invoices

Your business can be paid by direct deposit for the goods and services you sell to the government.

Find contact information for direct deposit for suppliers

Taxes

Use the CRA’s My Business Account to sign-up or update your banking information for direct deposit for refunds, rebates and overpayments of:

  • Payroll deductions
  • GST/HST
  • Corporate taxes
  • Excise taxes and other levies

Grants and contributions

If you are the recipient of a grant or contribution from the Government of Canada, your agreement with the department or agency should provide instructions on how to register for direct deposit. For questions, reach out to the contact listed in your contribution agreement.
