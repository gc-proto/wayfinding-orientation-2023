Forgot your GCKey password or username

Forgot your GCKey password or username

Choose the instructions for your situation:

If you added a recovery email address to GCKey:
  • select Forgot your username or Forgot your password on the Sign-in with GCkey page
  • if you've forgotten both, select Forgot your username on the Sign-in with GCkey page to have it emailed, then use Forgot your password
if you didn't add a recovery email address to GCKey
  • follow the steps to register for a new account
  • during this process, you may be able to recover your account or link existing applications to your new account
GCKey username and password revoked

Your GCKey username and password have been revoked or are missing, invalid or incomplete

If you haven't signed in with your GCKey username and password at least once in the last 2 years, they have been revoked and removed. You'll need to:

  • register for the service again
  • during this process, you may be able to recover your account or link existing applications to your new account

Switch your Sign-in partner

Use a different Sign-in partner

If you've moved to a new bank or credit union, and already sign in through Interac® Sign-in Partner, you may be able to switch:

  • select Switch My Sign-in Partner from the menu bar on the Interac® Sign-in Partnerpage

Browser error messages

Your browser shows an error message

Update your browser if you see one of these messages when you try to sign in:

  • Page Can’t Be Displayed
  • Cannot Establish a Secure Connection
  • Page Not Available

You can usually find the option to Update your browser in your browser menu, settings or preferences. The latest versions of browsers have security features to keep your personal information safe.

Use a modern web browser like Google Chrome, Safari, or Microsoft Edge, rather than Internet Explorer.
